NLUCloud AI & Society Review is not a news blog or a tool review site. It is an **editorial layer** on top of formal research.

Each paper on SSRN is **authoritative source material**. Cover essays here are **translations** — the same argument reframed for readers who will never read a 30-page working paper. The [Lexicon](/lexicon/) names the concepts; the [Research](/research/) section indexes the underlying working papers.

The program asks one question from multiple angles: **when AI makes generation cheap, what becomes scarce on the human side?** Judgment. Representation. Learning architecture. Waiting. Latency. Creative motive.

Essays stay free on NLUCloud.com. Optional [support on Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/nlucloud) helps keep the publication independent. [Subscribe](/subscribe/) for a monthly note when a new issue is published.
