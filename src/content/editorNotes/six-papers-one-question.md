Six papers on SSRN are not six separate projects. They are one program viewed from six entry points.

When external costs fall — generation, access, information — constraints migrate to the human side:

- **Representation** (Self-Distortion): Can you say what you actually mean?
- **Waiting** (AWT → ALT): Who pays in time and attention?
- **Judgment** (JAP): Who completes value and carries responsibility?
- **Motivation** (Reweighting): How are creative motives rewarded and reweighted?
- **Learning** (LMO): Which learning path fits this learner and this skill?

NLU Cloud exists to make this program readable without requiring readers to assemble it themselves from preprints.
