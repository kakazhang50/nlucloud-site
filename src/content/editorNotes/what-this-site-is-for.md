NLU Cloud is not a news blog or a tool review site. It is an **editorial layer** on top of formal research.

Each paper on SSRN is **authoritative source material**. Essays here are **translations** — the same argument reframed for readers who will never read a 30-page working paper. One paper can yield many essays, placed in different columns when the same idea speaks to language, cognition, society, education, humanities, or everyday life.

The research program asks a single question from multiple angles: **when AI makes generation cheap, what becomes scarce on the human side?** Waiting. Representation. Judgment. Learning paths. Creative motives. Daily rhythm.

This site will grow as the research grows. Papers are the well; essays are the water drawn from it — without limit, as long as the arguments remain faithful to the source.
