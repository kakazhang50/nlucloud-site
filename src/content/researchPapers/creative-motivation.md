This paper proposes a **triadic framework** of creative motivation (expression-driven, feedback-driven, outcome-driven) and the **motivational reweighting thesis**: generative AI reorganizes activation costs and institutional rewards of motives that have long coexisted.

It introduces **expression-weight restoration** for low-stakes, self-directed creation under reduced technical friction.
