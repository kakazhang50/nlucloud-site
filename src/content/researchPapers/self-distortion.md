When AI outputs feel generic or misaligned, the problem is often attributed to the model. This paper advances a **user-side account**: interaction quality depends on how faithfully internal cognition is represented in language.

Two constructs are introduced: **Expression Distortion Cost (EDC)** and **Self-Alignment Load (SAL)**, integrated in the **Authenticity–Cost Model**.
