Generative AI increases output abundance but intensifies the burden of **judgment**. This paper introduces **Judgment-as-Product (JAP)**: AI outputs are materials, not products, until a human authorizes their use under accountability.

The binding constraint of production shifts from information acquisition to judgment formation, integration, and articulation.
