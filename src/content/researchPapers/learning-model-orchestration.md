When information is abundant, the constraint shifts to **how learning happens**. This paper introduces **Learning Model Orchestration (LMO)** and argues that education should move from personalization alone to orchestrated learning across the life course.

It analyzes **orchestration inequality** — who gets access to better learning paths in AI-expanded education systems.
