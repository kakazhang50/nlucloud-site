Building on the AI Waiting Tax, this paper systematizes **the AI Latency Tax (ALT)** — latency as a socio-technical externality with implications for temporal justice, digital inequality, and governance in education, healthcare, and public services.

Waiting is no longer an empty interval but a site of extraction and stratification.
