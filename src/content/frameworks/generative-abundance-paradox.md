title: "Generative Abundance Paradox"
abbreviation: "GAP"

sourcePaper: "judgment-as-product"
lexiconNumber: 4

---

The **generative abundance paradox**: generation compresses production time but may **expand** decision time. The bottleneck moves from producing candidates to evaluating and authorizing one under limited human bandwidth.
