**Learning model scarcity** names the shift from knowledge scarcity to architecture scarcity. When facts, explanations, and generated content are cheap, what remains scarce is **how learning is structured** — which models of explanation, practice, feedback, and reflection are selected, sequenced, and combined.

LMO responds to this scarcity by treating learning design as orchestration, not content delivery.
