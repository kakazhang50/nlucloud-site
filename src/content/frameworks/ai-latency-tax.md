The **AI Latency Tax (ALT)** extends waiting from subjective experience to **structural inequality**. In LLM-era systems, delay is increasingly stratified by tier, context, and ability to pay.

ALT has three defining properties: **invisibility**, **cumulativeness**, and **asymmetry**.
