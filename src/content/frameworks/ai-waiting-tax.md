The **AI Waiting Tax (AWT)** names the burden of waiting for AI systems to respond. It is not merely annoyance at slow servers. It is the cost of **suspended attention** — time in which the user cannot fully proceed, disengage, or use the interval productively without fragmentation.

AWT is the experiential foundation for the structural **AI Latency Tax (ALT)**.
