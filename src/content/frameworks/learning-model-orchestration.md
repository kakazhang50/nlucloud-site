**Learning Model Orchestration (LMO)** is the intentional design of how a learner moves among learning models — explanation, questioning, practice, feedback, simulation, reflection — rather than delivering more content within a single instructional logic.

When personalization adjusts pace inside one model, orchestration designs **across** models under cognitive constraint.
