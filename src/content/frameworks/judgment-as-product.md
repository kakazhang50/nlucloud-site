**Judgment-as-Product (JAP)** holds that AI-generated outputs are **materials**, not **products**, until a human selects, validates, adopts, and accepts responsibility for consequences.

When production is cheap, the binding constraint shifts to judgment burden, decision latency, and responsibility concentration.
