**Expression-weight restoration** describes the renewed practical significance of low-stakes, self-directed creation when technical friction drops. Images, text, and short media become easier to produce — so expression-driven making can matter again outside commodity markets and professional gatekeeping.

This is not a claim that everyone becomes an artist. It is a claim that **expression motive** can be reactivated when making no longer requires heavy skill or institutional permission.
