**Orchestration inequality** is the shift from possession to configuration: who can convert access to AI into coherent learning systems — not merely who owns devices or subscriptions.

One learner uses AI as a shortcut machine; another uses it as an orchestration layer. The tool is the same; outcomes diverge.
