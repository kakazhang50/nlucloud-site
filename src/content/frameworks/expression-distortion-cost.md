---
title: "Expression Distortion Cost"
abbreviation: "EDC"
description: "Performance loss when expressed input to AI diverges from the user's underlying goals, constraints, and preferences."
sourcePaper: "self-distortion"
status: published
---

**Expression Distortion Cost (EDC)** captures the error side of human–AI interaction: outputs may be fluent yet structurally misaligned because the input did not faithfully represent the user's operative internal state.

EDC pairs with **Self-Alignment Load (SAL)** in the Authenticity–Cost Model.
